About COMAC

COMAC, or Commercial Aircraft Corporation of China, Ltd., is a central player in China's strategic plan to establish a significant presence in the global aerospace industry. Founded in 2008, COMAC is tasked with the development and production of jet aircraft that can compete on the international stage.

Our Aircraft

C919

learn more

The C919 is a single-aisle jetliner designed to compete directly with the Airbus A320neo and Boeing 737 MAX series. With a standard range of 4,075 to 5,555 kilometers, the C919 aims to meet the growing demand for commercial air travel in China and abroad.

ARJ21

learn more

The ARJ21, or Advanced Regional Jet for the 21st Century, is COMAC's first foray into commercial aircraft manufacturing. Designed to serve short to medium-haul routes, the ARJ21 is a symbol of China's ambitions in the regional jet market.
